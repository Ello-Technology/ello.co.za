Corporate Profile



ELLO TECHNOLOGY, established in 2002, is a South African-based innovative Information and Communications Technology (ICT) Company that is focused on providing ICT Infrastructure Management to corporate organizations, and, in particular the SME Sector. With a national foot print and a workforce of over 40, Ello Technology are able to provide reliable solutions and support for multi-branch organizations.

Garry Cahay - Chief Executive Officer

The driving force and public face of the business, Garry Cahay founded Ello Technology in 2002. With the support of loyal clients and a strong Management Team, Garry has been involved in overseeing and managing the expansion of Ello Technology, while keeping track with the ever changing world of technology. Garry has a very hand on approach and keeps himself centrally involved in Technical deployments on a daily basis. Garry believes that you need to “lead by example” and in fact that is exactly their slogan today. Garry’s passion for getting things right the first time is what drives him to find reliable solutions.

Amber Smit - Managing Director

After joining Ello Technology at the beginning of 2008, Amber Smit stands at the forefront of the fastest growing ICT company in Southern Africa. Heading up the operational requirements of Ello and embracing the core values of integrity, innovation, and growth, Amber is committed to ensuring that each client receives world class service. By monitoring client trends and needs, we are able to predict and plan to ensure that each client maximizes their time efficiently and avoid wasted downtime. Service excellence and ethics is key to Amber’s success story
