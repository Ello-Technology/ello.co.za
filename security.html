Security Management

Security Management aims to ensure the confidentiality, integrity and availability of an organization’s information, data and IT services
The recent events associated with the ‘heartbleed, ransomware, wannacry’ virus that swept through the tech world is an example of how security breaches can affect certain security measures that can lead to the breaching of your organizations sensitive personal information thereby negatively affecting your business. Therefore the next step in server and firewall management is adequately securing your servers and firewalls, and checking its health by:

Integrating periodic security audits and
Carrying out the necessary security, kernel, and software updates
Preventive Services and Measures

Nothing is infallible and from time to time your servers may crash or be affected negatively by viral attacks therefore, it is imperative that every server should instill preventive measures such as initiating:

A server backup and restoration process
Server migration measures
Disaster Recovery and Software support.

We help our clients take control of security by working with your organization. Ello Technology Security Management focuses on how to create the right security level then implements its findings throughout the business, creating lasting and controllable measures. Security management is a method to identify the optimal security level and focus on security work in areas where the greatest benefits can be achieved.
Security management can be applied throughout the business, such as in development projects, in operational processes or in an isolated system. Our working method is characterized by practical implementation. We concentrate the larger part of our work on teaching the customer to work with decentralized security management in their organization. Our operational approach results in immediate and beneficial effects for the customer.
